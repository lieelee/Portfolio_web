About Me

Hello there! I’m Yeonuk, and I’m an entry level software engineer based in the bay area, CA. Last year, I received my bachelor's degree in International Finance with a minor in Spanish and am now pursuing a degree in computer science at the University of People.

I come from diverse backgrounds. While learning finance in school, I also studied Official Development Assistance known as Overseas Aids. In 2015, I began working as an Ambassador for UN-Habitat in South Korea. After that, I worked as a publicist for a non-profit organization in 2016. My main role in both jobs was marketing and organizing the events, and communicating with people.

My passion in computer science began when I was introduced to the C programming language a few years ago. I like every stage of the process of solving difficulties that at first appeared impossible to overcome. The idea of becoming a creator and turning intangible thoughts into tangible items inspired me to jump into the field.
